Design

Our interior design services begin with our experienced and educated design team meeting with the client to establish expectations and needs for their project. Much attention is focused on value and budgetary issues to ensure the client is getting exactly what they expect and can afford. Based on the project our team may select finishes, provide detailed specification packages, space plan, provide floor plans, provide 3d renderings, and/or specify furniture. The information is then presented to the client before any orders are processed ensuring a satisfied client and avoids any surprises.

Commercial Furniture

Specifying the proper finishes, furniture & accessories for your interior will play a key role in employee productivity and in your client's overall impression of your business. We specify, supply, and install commercial furniture from hundreds of vendors so we can meet your style and budget. Our design team is capable of handling projects of all sizes; from one piece of furniture to an entire remodel.

Flooring

We specify, supply, and install a variety of flooring material. Whether you're looking for carpet tile, broadloom, luxury vinyl tile, VCT, ceramic tile, porcelain tile, or rubber flooring we can help.

Installation

Our installation partners are constructed of highly skilled and experienced professionals. They are well versed in all aspects of the furniture and floor covering installation process allowing us to offer the best service possible.

Office Relocation

If you are moving to a new building we can inventory and move your existing office furniture and install it at your new location. Furniture disposal is available if needed.
